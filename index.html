Profile

I bridge teaching, data science, and algorithmic thinking.

I am a computer science educator with a data science background. My work sits at the intersection of classroom teaching, analytical reasoning, and practical programming. I like helping students move from confusion to structure: first by making the idea visible, then by turning it into code, and finally by testing it against real examples.

Before teaching full time, I worked with reliability engineering, public-health data, geospatial analysis, and statistical consulting. That experience shapes how I teach: I treat computer science as a way to model problems, inspect evidence, and build disciplined habits of thinking.
